The GLB files and shared colormap texture in this directory are from Kenney's
Survival Kit 2.0, distributed under Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal
(CC0 1.0):
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

Creator and source:

- Kenney (https://kenney.nl/)
- Survival Kit (https://kenney.nl/assets/survival-kit)
- Release date: 2024-04-03

Files used by this project:

- kenney-tool-hatchet.glb
  Renamed from `Models/GLB format/tool-axe.glb`.
- kenney-tool-pickaxe.glb
  Renamed from `Models/GLB format/tool-pickaxe.glb`.
- Textures/colormap.png

Kenney permits personal, educational, and commercial use. Attribution is not
required by CC0, but this record is kept with the assets so their provenance
